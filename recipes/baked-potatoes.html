Baked Potatoes

Image source: BBC Good Food

Description

A simple recipe that's easy on the wallet to make fluffy, crispy baked potatoes that can serve up to 8 people. These potatoes can also be served alongside cheese, baked beans or soured cream.

Ingredients

  • 8 large baking potatoes
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 300ml soured cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat your oven to 220C/200C fan/gas 7.
  2. Brush some oil over each potato and place these on the top shelf in the oven.
  3. Bake the potatoes for 20 mins, then turn the oven down to 190C/170C fan/gas 5 for an additional 45-60 mins until it's crispy outside and soft inside.
  4. Form a cross in each potato's centre like the image above.
  5. If you'd like, place the soured cream on top of the potatoes and serve.
